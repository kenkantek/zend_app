SolGold Overview

SolGold was listed on the London Stock Exchange (AIM code : SOLG) on 10 February 2006.
SolGold is headed up by experienced and committed mining industry professionals with demonstratable track records of mineral discovery and providing valuable shareholder return. The Board of Directors include accomplished professionals with experience in exploration, mine development, investment, finance and law. The Board and Management have significant vested interests in the Company, holding approximately 17.5% of its issued share capital. The Company currently has 284,623,489 shares in issue.

SolGold has Exploration tenements in Queensland, Australia, Ecuador and the Solomon Islands. Select our Project Areas tab for more detailed information.
